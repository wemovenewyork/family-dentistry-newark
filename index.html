Meet your dentist

Following in his father's footsteps

Dr. Colden Raines Jr. knew from an early age that he wanted to become a dentist — just like his father before him. That early sense of purpose shaped a career built on prevention, patient education, and treating every person who sits in his chair with genuine care.

A graduate of Howard University, Dr. Raines has spent his career caring for Newark families on Clinton Avenue. He believes the best dentistry is the kind that prevents problems before they start, and that patients deserve to understand every recommendation before any treatment begins.

Outside the practice, Dr. Raines is an oil painter and a longtime community advocate — once recognized in The New York Times for his efforts to make Newark a safer place to live. That same commitment to the neighborhood shows up in how he runs his practice.

Comprehensive care under one roof

From routine cleanings to restoring a damaged smile, Dr. Raines and our team offer a full range of general and family dental services — so you can get the care you need in a place you already trust.

Preventive & family dentistry

The foundation of lifelong oral health. Regular exams, professional cleanings, and digital X-rays help us catch small issues before they become big ones — for every member of your family.

Routine exams Professional cleanings Digital X-rays Children's dentistry

Restorative dentistry

When a tooth is damaged or decayed, we restore both its function and appearance. Dr. Raines favors conservative treatment that preserves your natural teeth whenever possible.

Tooth-colored fillings Crowns Bridges

Cosmetic dentistry

If you feel self-conscious about your smile, we can help. Our professional teeth whitening lifts stains from coffee, aging, and everyday life for noticeable, long-lasting results.

Professional whitening Smile improvements

Gum & periodontal care

Healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy mouth. We evaluate and treat gum disease — the leading cause of tooth loss in adults — with care tailored to your needs.

Scaling & root planing Deep cleanings Gum & bone grafts

Tooth extractions

When a tooth can't be saved, Dr. Raines performs gentle extractions in a calm, caring environment — taking the time to explain the procedure and answer every question so you feel at ease.

Simple extractions Comfort-focused care

Early detection screening

Many serious conditions are far easier to manage when caught early. As part of your routine visits, we screen for signs of trouble so concerns can be addressed before they grow.

Oral health screening Routine monitoring

Welcome — we're glad you're here

We want your first visit to feel comfortable and unhurried. Here's exactly what to expect, so there are no surprises when you arrive.

Your first visit

What to expect, step by step

1

A warm welcome

Our front-desk team will greet you, help with any remaining paperwork, and get you settled in.

2

A thorough exam

Dr. Raines completes a comprehensive exam, including digital X-rays, to get a complete picture of your oral health.

3

A one-on-one consultation

You'll sit down with Dr. Raines to discuss his findings, your goals and concerns, and any treatment options — with time for every question.

4

A clear plan forward

We'll review your dental benefits and costs upfront, so you leave with a personalized care plan you fully understand.

Insurance

We accept most major dental insurance plans and will file claims on your behalf as a courtesy. To keep your visit smooth, please call ahead so we can verify your coverage before you arrive.

Plans accepted include Blue Cross Blue Shield, Guardian, and Oscar, among others. Call to confirm your specific plan.

Financing & payment

No insurance? No problem. We offer flexible payment options so cost never stands between you and the care you need.

Our team will walk through all available options with you and answer any billing questions before treatment begins.

Common questions

New patient FAQs

How often should I see the dentist?
For most patients, a checkup and cleaning every six months is the right rhythm to keep small problems small. Dr. Raines will let you know if your situation calls for a different schedule.
Are you accepting new patients?
Yes — Dr. Raines is currently welcoming new patients of all ages. Call us at (973) 824-7409 or request an appointment online to get started.
Do you see children?
Absolutely. As a family practice, we care for every generation — from a child's first visit to grandparents — all under one roof.
What if I have dental anxiety?
You're in good company, and you're in good hands. Dr. Raines is known for an unhurried, gentle approach — he'll explain each step and go at a pace that keeps you comfortable.
