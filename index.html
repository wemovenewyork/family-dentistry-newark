The person behind the practice

He grew up watching his father do this work

Dr. Raines knew early that he'd follow his father into dentistry. That inheritance shows up in how he practices — unhurried exams, plain-language explanations, and a strong belief that the best treatment is the one you never need.

Away from the chair he's an oil painter, and a community advocate once recognized in The New York Times for his work to make Newark safer. The same investment in this city shapes the way he runs his practice.
