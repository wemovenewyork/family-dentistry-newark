Comprehensive care under one roof

From routine cleanings to restoring a damaged smile, Dr. Raines and our team offer a full range of general and family dental services — so you can get the care you need in a place you already trust.

Preventive & family dentistry

The foundation of lifelong oral health. Regular exams, professional cleanings, and digital X-rays help us catch small issues before they become big ones — for every member of your family.

Routine exams Professional cleanings Digital X-rays Children's dentistry

Restorative dentistry

When a tooth is damaged or decayed, we restore both its function and appearance. Dr. Raines favors conservative treatment that preserves your natural teeth whenever possible.

Tooth-colored fillings Crowns Bridges

Cosmetic dentistry

If you feel self-conscious about your smile, we can help. Our professional teeth whitening lifts stains from coffee, aging, and everyday life for noticeable, long-lasting results.

Professional whitening Smile improvements

Gum & periodontal care

Healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy mouth. We evaluate and treat gum disease — the leading cause of tooth loss in adults — with care tailored to your needs.

Scaling & root planing Deep cleanings Gum & bone grafts

Tooth extractions

When a tooth can't be saved, Dr. Raines performs gentle extractions in a calm, caring environment — taking the time to explain the procedure and answer every question so you feel at ease.

Simple extractions Comfort-focused care

Early detection screening

Many serious conditions are far easier to manage when caught early. As part of your routine visits, we screen for signs of trouble so concerns can be addressed before they grow.

Oral health screening Routine monitoring

Not sure what you need? That's what your first visit is for. Dr. Raines will complete a thorough exam, walk you through what he finds, and recommend only the care that's right for you — no pressure, no surprises.
